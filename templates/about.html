關於我們 About Us

本平台由學生發起，為學生而建。 升學申請過程往往資訊不透明、經驗難以取得，許多學生只能依賴零碎資訊或行銷內容來做出重要決定。我們相信，學生應該能夠接觸到更真實、可參考的升學結果與經驗。 本平台讓學生能夠匿名、自願地分享自己的申請結果，包括錄取、拒絕、候補與最終入學情況，並可選擇性提供背景資訊。隨著時間累積，這些資料將形成一個由學生共同參與與維護的參考資料庫，幫助後來者更理性地理解升學可能性。 本計畫始於澳門城市大學，長期目標是逐步建立一個由學生共同擁有的升學資訊平台，從社群出發，穩定發展。 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- This platform was created by students, for students. The university application process is often opaque, with limited access to real outcomes and experiences. Many applicants are forced to rely on fragmented information or marketing-driven narratives. We believe students deserve clearer and more trustworthy references. Our platform allows students to anonymously and voluntarily share their application outcomes, including offers, rejections, waitlists, and final enrollment decisions, along with optional background information. Over time, these contributions form a student-owned dataset that helps future applicants better understand realistic possibilities. This project began at City University of Macau, with the long-term vision of building a scalable, student-owned admissions transparency platform, growing carefully from one community to another.
